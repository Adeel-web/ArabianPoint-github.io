It Starts

Around The Fire

AraBian Point Resturant Is The Herat Of Comunity

Think back to a time when you gathered around a fire with friends and family.
The smell of brewing coffee fills your nostrils. The children help set the table as
the women put the final touches to the meal. Uncles puff on shisha while crowding
around a table playing cards and discussing the news of the day. Do you remember?

That is what Nar Restaurant offers you.

The idea behind Nar Restaurant is as old as the Levant.
Built by the traditions that shape our culture. A place where you can bring your friends and
family to recreate your best memories of “home.”

IT BEGAN LONG AGO

Our Story

Our story began long ago, with fire at its heart. The morning coffee
brewing, and neighbors’ chatter marking the day’s start.

While the table is being set and lunch is being prepared, loved
ones wait by the fire for the feast and stories to be shared.

As someone wonders if the shisha’s lit, and warmth mixes
with cheer, we can’t wait to welcome you in our midst. Everything’s ready for you here.
